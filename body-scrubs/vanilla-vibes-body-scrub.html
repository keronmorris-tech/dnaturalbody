A warm, vanilla-sugar body scrub that gently buffs away dull, flaky skin while wrapping your body in a cozy, dessert-like scent. Skin feels soft, smooth, and touchably sweet after every rinse.

Benefits

This creamy sugar scrub exfoliates without stripping, helping to smooth rough patches while leaving behind soft, moisturized skin and a sweet vanilla trail. Gently removes dead skin build-up for a more even texture. Shea butter and oils help keep skin cushioned and nourished. Perfect prep step before shaving or applying body butter.

Ingredients

Sugar, Glycerin, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Fragrance (Parfum), and a gentle preservative system to keep your scrub fresh.

Key skin benefits:
Sugar – provides gentle physical exfoliation to polish away dullness.
Shea Butter – helps replenish moisture and comfort dry areas.
Jojoba & Sweet Almond Oils – add a silky finish and help lock in hydration.

How to Use

On damp skin, massage a small amount in circular motions, focusing on areas like legs, arms, and elbows. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry. Follow with your favorite body yogurt or body butter for extra glow. Use 2–3 times per week.
