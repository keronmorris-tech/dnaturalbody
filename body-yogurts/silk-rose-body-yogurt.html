Benefits

Benefits
Lightweight rose-scented hydration that absorbs quickly.
Jojoba and shea help soften rough, dry patches for a silky feel.
Broccoli seed oil gives a subtle, satin finish without greasiness.
Perfect for layering under floral fragrances or wearing on its own.

Ingredients

Ingredients
Glycerin, Xanthan Gum, Aqua (Distilled Water), Propanediol (1,3),
Glyceryl Stearate, PEG-100 Stearate, Sodium Carbomer,
Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil,
Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter,
Brassica Oleracea Italica (Broccoli) Seed Oil,
Propylene Glycol (and) Diazolidinyl Urea (and)

Key skin benefits:
Glycerin
– attracts water into the skin for a bouncy, hydrated feel.
Jojoba Oil
– mimics skin’s natural oils to soften without clogging pores.
Shea Butter
– nourishes dry areas and supports the skin barrier.
Broccoli Seed Oil
– leaves skin feeling smooth with a silky sheen.
Aloe Vera
– calms and comforts freshly cleansed or shaved skin.

How to Use

How to Use
Smooth onto clean, slightly damp skin after showering.
Massage in until fully absorbed, focusing on arms, chest,
and neck for a soft rose veil. Use daily for best results.
